How You Can Help

Donate Funds

  • Donate to verified relief organizations.
  • Specify if funds are for emergency or recovery efforts.
  • Participate in community fundraising events.
Donate Now

Volunteering

  • Help through official, recognized organizations.
  • Use skills such as medical care, translation, or logistics.
  • Assist with local charity drives and community events.

Spread Awareness

  • Share verified emergency information, including Turkish versions.
  • Teach communities how to respond before and after aftershocks.
  • Promote online awareness campaigns.
  • Share evacuation routes & emergency contacts.

Crisis Stages

Emergency Phase
  • Immediate access to water, shelter, and food.
  • First aid and medical support.
  • Evacuation and temporary housing.
Recovery Phase
  • Rebuilding homes and infrastructure.
  • Strengthening buildings for safety.
  • Long-term community programs and mental health support.
Preparedness Phase
  • Creating family emergency plans.
  • Building and updating supply kits.
  • Training volunteers in CPR & emergency response.
